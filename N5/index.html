About Baseball

  • Name: Reed Sparling
  • Height: 5'10
  • Age: 18 Years
  • Weight: 165
  • Hometown: San Jose, California

This page is dedicated to my baseball playing career. Much of my life has been all about baseball, and the hours and effort ai have put in to becoming a better player. Unfortunately, in my senior season I tore my ACL. This moment ended my competitive playing career. I wanted to dedicate this page to playing baseball and growing up with the game. Baseball has provided me with so many memories and friends over the years and it is very important to reflect on all of the great years I have had playing the game.

I want this site to serve as a memoir to everyhthing I accomplished and everything baseball has taught me over the years.

My Injuries

  • Partially Torn Achilles

    When: 2014

    I suffered this injury sliding into second base.

  • Torn Labrum

    When: 2016

    This injury was caused by overthrowing and throwing to many curveballs. I had to get surgery and missed all of freshman baseball.

  • Torn ACL

    When: 2019

    This injury I suffered this year changing directions on the basepaths. After a great start to my senior season, I had to get surgery and miss all of the year.
